Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Sweet Tradition

Since 1978, Central Sweet has been the heartbeat of celebrations across families and generations. Born in the heart of the city, our mithai shop grew from a modest kitchen into a beloved institution — not just selling sweets, but crafting memories.

Every Kaju Katli is rolled to the perfect thickness. Every Gulab Jamun is soaked overnight in rose-kissed syrup. We use no preservatives, no shortcuts — only the finest saffron from Kashmir, the purest milk, and recipes whispered down through generations of our family.

Our master halwais begin work before sunrise so that every piece you taste carries the warmth of a freshly made morning treat — because true luxury is simply excellence, every single day.
